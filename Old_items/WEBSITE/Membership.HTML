So, What is the project about?



It is a project which aims to provide sanitation, healthcare and various other utilities to different parts of the world which require it. We will also provide cleaner environment, which has -
  1. Less Air,Water and Noise Pollution
  2. Preservation of Natural Resources
  3. No Deforestation
  4. Unlittered Public Facilities,such as parks, roads etc.


Why join us?



You might have come across various organistions working towards sanitation in India, but we have our different methods. We believe in interacting with the public to work with public for the public. After joining this organisation, you will get different notifications, announcements and requests related to the cleanliness of the streets of India.

We will allow people to connect with each other and be aware about welfare of our country and also our society. All you will have to do is provide us the details of the places which are remote and are not provided with sanitation and cleanliness. You can also send pictures of the area you want to be modified.
