Forbidden Verses

Forbidden Verses is our little part to bring art to everyone else. This community started in October 2018 with a little open mic is ever growing. Through our events we aim to give a space to artists to truly express themselves without any conditions and try to make art more accessible for all. There are poems that can change the world, paintings that build new ones, photography that captures both the ugly and the beautiful and music which sets this change into motion, and our aim is to bring these glorious artforms under one roof.

Our community is ever growing with new art forms being experimented each event and our audiences come not only to listen, but to become parts of little stories themselves. We also believe current social situations can never be divorced from the art we create, hence, we provide a safe space for these difficult dialogues to actually be facilitated. We are open to collaborations with any organisation that aims at making the skyline of Delhi and beyond, happier with art, expression and any civic engagement.

Some general guidelines

  • Each performer will be given maximum 5 minutes
  • You're allowed to read works by different writers and share your stories but due credit should be given to the original artist.
  • There are no boundaries to literature and hence we don't want to keep any too.
  • We have 1 mic (with stand) and 2 amplifiers. If you require anything else please inform us.
  • The event will only be a success with your co-operation. Please feel free to connect with us for any query that you have.
  • An Entry fee of 50 has to be paid at the venue on the day of the event.
