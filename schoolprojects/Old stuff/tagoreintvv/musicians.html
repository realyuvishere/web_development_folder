Musicians Workshop
The event will be hosted by the Chilean music group Ensamble Transatlántico de Folk Chileno and some of the best local Indian artists in Delhi on 24th October 2016. You will learn
  • Composition of music

  • Arrangement of pieces of music

  • Chilean Folk rhythms

  • History of music in Chile
